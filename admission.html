The Requirements

To apply for TESDA scholarship (FREE tuition):

  • Must be 18 to 25 years old.
  • 4 pieces of 2x2 ID picture (with white background)
  • 4 pieces of 1x1 ID picture (with white background)
  • Barangay Clearance or Police Clearance (either one)
  • Copy of Form 138 and Form 137 or ALS Certificate
  • Certification of Indigency
  • NSO Birth Certificate
